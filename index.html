Welcome to K-PAI Nexus!

K-PAI Nexus is a next-generation Silicon Valley community exploring AI's profound impact on technology, economy, society, ethics, and humanity—and translating that exploration into real-world action.

We have evolved into the premier Silicon Valley AI community bridging Korean and global tech ecosystems. Our community has grown and united by a shared conviction – AI must advance human flourishing, not diminish it.

Everything we do is guided by three core pillars!

  • Explore & Lead — World-class forums, the AI & Humanity Council, and thought leadership shaping AI's most consequential questions
  • Elevate Community — Mentorship, startup showcases, professional connections, and leadership opportunities that amplify every voice
  • Give Back to Society — Policy engagement, academic partnerships, and public discourse ensuring AI's benefits reach beyond Silicon Valley

Our discussions span the full spectrum of AI's impact—from semiconductor design and autonomous systems to medical AI, legal ethics, climate sustainability, and the future of work—featuring speakers and collaborators from Amazon, OpenAI, Google, Stanford, Seoul National University, Asan Medical Center, and beyond.

We ARE co-hosting a lot with our partners

Within 2026, K-PAI Nexus will co-host diverse Forums with K-BioX, KOTRA Silicon Valley, Consulate General of Republic of Korea, San Francisco, and Korean American Bar Association of Northern California (KABANC); specific months not assigned yet, but will be soon!

Vision Statement

A world where artificial intelligence and human agency create harmonious counterpoint—each maintaining their essential characteristics while together composing a future that amplifies human dignity, protects individual autonomy, and unlocks unprecedented innovation!

Mission Statement

K-PAI Nexus fosters a pioneering community of professionals dedicated to developing AI solutions through cross-disciplinary collaboration, bridging technical innovation with legal frameworks and humanistic principles to create AI systems that serve humanity while protecting individual privacy!
